# About & Health Note

## What ProteinPlate is

ProteinPlate is a free, open guide built by analysing dozens of real, home-cooked Indian meals and distilling them into one repeatable template. It is **general food and wellness information — not medical or dietetic advice.**

## An honest health note

- **Your real targets are personal.** A safe calorie deficit and protein target depend on your age, weight, activity, and any medical conditions. A **registered dietitian** can set these precisely.
- **High protein + specific conditions:** very high protein needs caution with **kidney disease**; people with **diabetes** should monitor how legumes and carbs affect their glucose.
- **Balanced, not extreme.** This style of eating — varied vegetables, lean protein, fibre, healthy fats, minimal sugar and refined carbs — is genuinely good. Keep it sustainable rather than punishing.
- **Keep a human in the loop.** Talk to a doctor or dietitian before starting keto or a steep calorie cut.

## The numbers

Grams and calories throughout the site are **reasonable estimates** for planning, read from photographs and standard food values. They are not laboratory-measured. Treat them as a starting point, not a prescription.
